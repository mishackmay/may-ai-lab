Why is pricing in Rands and not USD? ▼
We built May AI Lab specifically for African businesses. Paying in USD through foreign platforms creates unnecessary barriers. Our ZAR pricing means no surprise exchange rate fees and no international transaction charges.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel whenever you want and you keep access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods will you accept? ▼
We'll accept all major South African payment methods through PayFast — credit/debit cards, EFT, Ozow instant EFT, and SnapScan.
Is my data stored in South Africa? ▼
We take data privacy seriously and comply with POPIA. Your business data is handled securely and we never sell it to third parties.
